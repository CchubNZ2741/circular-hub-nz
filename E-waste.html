A Chain of Hidden Dangers

At each stage of this journey, improperly handled e-waste creates real risks that affect our safety and our environment.

Whose Problem Is It?

It's a shared community responsibility.

From the families and businesses that create the waste, to the systems we use to manage it, we all have a role to play. The good news is that this means we also have the shared power to solve it.

How We Can Solve The Problem

It Starts With a Simple Choice

The solution begins before the rubbish truck even arrives. By choosing to separate our e-waste from our general rubbish, we can turn a hazardous liability into a valuable resource.

1. We Protect Our Community

Separating e-waste, especially batteries, prevents fires in our homes and in collection trucks, making our community safer for everyone.

2. We Create Value

By collecting e-waste properly, we can safely recover the valuable metals inside, a process called "urban mining," which is far better for our planet than traditional mining.

Let's Build a Safer, More Sustainable Future.

Change starts with awareness. By understanding the e-waste problem, we can take the first steps together to build a circular economy right here in our community.
