Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Circular Hub NZ, a platform operated by CL Regen Global. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about what we do with it. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your information in compliance with New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, business name, email address, and phone number.
  • Service Request Details: Information you provide about your e-waste, including location, type, and volume.
  • Initiative Listing Details: Information you provide about your organisation for inclusion in our directory.

3. How We Use Your Information

The primary purpose for collecting this information is to operate our platform and facilitate connections within the circular economy. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Connect you with appropriate service providers for your e-waste needs.
  • List your organisation or initiative in our public directory.
  • Communicate with you regarding your submission or our services.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

To fulfill our service, we will share your information with relevant third parties. By submitting a form, you consent to this sharing.

  • If you submit an "E-Waste Service Request," we will share the details of your request, including your contact information, with one or more service providers listed in our directory who we believe can best assist you.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is collected and stored securely through our web hosting provider, Netlify. We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access and request the correction of any personal information we hold about you. If you wish to do so, please contact us using the details below.
